About Me

In the crucible of knowledge and creativity, I forge my path. Like Guts wielding his Dragonslayer, I approach each discipline with relentless determination and precision.

My journey began in the halls of academia, where I studied the fundamental laws that govern our universe through physics and mathematics. Yet, I soon realized that the most profound mysteries lie within the human mind itself, leading me to explore the intricate workings of neurology.

Parallel to my scientific pursuits, I've maintained the soul of an artist. My drawings capture both the beauty and darkness of existence, much like Kentaro Miura's masterful illustrations in Berserk. This duality of left-brain and right-brain thinking allows me to approach problems with unique perspectives.

I believe true understanding comes from the synthesis of seemingly disparate fields. Just as the Brand in Berserk connects two worlds, I seek to bridge the gap between art and science.
